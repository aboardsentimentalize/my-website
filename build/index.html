Welcome

Lily Lourigan is an actor and arts administrator based in Chicago. She has performed at Steppenwolf Theatre, Goodman Theatre, and A Red Orchid Theatre, among other major Chicago venues.

A 2023 3Arts Make a Wave grantee and School at Steppenwolf graduate, Stephanie is represented by Gray Talent Group. She currently serves as Marketing & Development Director at A Red Orchid Theatre.

Her extensive theater credits showcase her versatility across major Chicago venues, contributing to the vibrant theatrical landscape of the city.
