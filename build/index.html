Welcome

Lily Lourigan is an emerging actor based in Chicago and Milwaukee. She has performed at Bramble Theatre, Acacia Theatre, and Next Act Theatre (upcoming).

After having just graduated from Carthage College with a BA in Theatre Performance, she is excited to step into the professional world of theatre.

Notable credits include Kitty in Georgiana & Kitty with Acacia Theatre, Merna u/s in Rooted with Bramble Theatre, and Anna in Life Cycle of a Star at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2025.
